

# Reserved Characters

- `$` Dollar
- `&` Ampersand
- `+` Plus
- `,` Comma
- `/` Forward Slash
- `:` Colon
- `;` Semicolon
- `=` Equals
- `?` Question Mark
- `@` At 

# Unsafe Characters
- `"` Quotation Mark
- ` ` Space 
- `<` Less Than 
- `>` Greater Than 
- `#` Pound 
- `%` Percent 
- `{` Left Brace 
- `}` Right Brace 
- `\` Back Slash 
- `|` Pipe 
- `^` Caret 
- `~` Tilde 
- `[` Left Bracket 
- `]` Right Bracket 
- ``` ` ```Grave
